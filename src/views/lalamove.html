Phone Number Masking from Lalamove
— Written 2021 —
A pioneer in urban logistics that redefines the delivery landscape with its advanced app-based platform, connecting a vast network of professional drivers to facilitate swift and efficient urban logistics.
MY ROLE
As Product Manager for the platform, I worked within the communication & platform team to make the feature from conception to launch. I conducted research & quantitative analysis, managed customer onboarding, oversaw the sub product team of 3 people.
EVOLUTION OF THE PRODUCT
Lalamove as a prominent player in the logistics and delivery industry, has continually evolved its technology to enhance user privacy and security.

A significant advancement in this regard has been the implementation of phone number masking within its app. This feature, introduced to safeguard the personal contact details of both customers and drivers, works by creating a unique, temporary phone number for each transaction.

When a customer and a driver need to communicate, they can do so through this temporary number, ensuring that their actual phone numbers remain undisclosed. This development not only fortifies privacy but also builds trust in the platform, as it addresses concerns related to the sharing of personal information.

The implementation of phone number masking is a reflection of the company's commitment to user security, adapting to the changing landscape of digital communication and privacy needs.
KEY METRICS
- 70% of decrease in fraud rate due to misuse of phone number exposure.

- Attaining an average of 80% user adoption rate on this feature globally.

- Ensure that at least 75% of the feedback received is positive on it.
LEARNINGS
- How to work on global scale feature and perform the proper release strategy accordingly to minimize risk and costs.

- How to work with cross product teams (user & driver teams) to launch and build the feature to meet the timeline, together with alignment with legal & compliance teams.

- How to build communication and security related feature by integraing with telecommunication services and overcoming technical challenges, with complex use cases and scenario from a logistics product.
